Overview

A Shopify website for Little Pond Learning Center, an educational institution offering enrichment programs for young learners. The work covered the full design-to-build pipeline — Figma wireframes for the homepage and About Us pages, followed by implementation using Shopify with custom HTML and CSS. The site presents the center's academic programs including Filipino, Math, Reading, UPKAT Review, Forest Playgroup, and Enrichment tracks, and integrates product management for an accompanying books catalog via the Shopify admin.
